# Roadmap

> **Stub file.** Phase-level plan — not sprint-level. Replace with your project's real phases.

## Current Phase

**Phase name**: [e.g., "Foundation", "MVP", "Scale"]
**Goal**: One-sentence outcome that defines done for this phase.
**Timeline**: rough date range, not a hard deadline.
**Exit criteria**: measurable conditions that mean the phase is complete.

## Next Phase

What comes after the current phase ends. Why this order.

## Later

Phases that aren't scheduled but are committed-in-principle.

## Explicitly deferred

Work that's been considered and deliberately *not* scheduled. Include the reason.

## Update cadence

Update monthly or after any major pivot. Stale roadmaps are worse than no roadmap because agents will plan against them.
